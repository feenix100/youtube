Button Lab

These buttons are designed as reusable “recipes.” In exciting mode they gain stronger glow, depth, and a cursor-tracking spotlight. Two effects are driven by tiny JS: pointer-follow glow + magnetic hover.

Stylized Buttons

Hover each. Move your cursor across the button surface.
Tip: Reuse any button by copying its class + the CSS block: .b (base) + one variant (aurora / plasma / holo / circuit / glitch / candy). For cursor-follow glow + magnetic hover, keep the JS below.
