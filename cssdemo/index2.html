Footer Section

Hover Effects (Cards)

These are generic card hover effects. Replace the text with anything: images, icons, or content.

Lift + Shadow
Lift on Hover
Card rises slightly with stronger shadow. Great for clickable tiles or feature boxes.
Classes: hover-card hover-lift
Zoom In
Zoom on Hover
Light scale-up animation. Easy to combine with images or thumbnails.
Classes: hover-card hover-zoom
3D Tilt
Tilt on Hover
Subtle 3D tilt effect using rotate3d. Feels interactive without being too much.
Classes: hover-card hover-tilt
Blurred Background
Blurred Glass
Uses backdrop-filter to blur whatever is behind the card (supported in modern browsers).
Classes: hover-card hover-blur-bg
