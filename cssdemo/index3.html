This shows how to use transition on whole sections or panels. Hover each panel to see it expand and change style.

Intro Section
This could be your hero / landing section. On hover it grows slightly and gets a brighter background.
Content Section
Use transition on flex, background, and box-shadow to create smooth shifts between sections.
Footer Section
You can copy these cards and use them as full-width sections with hover or focus states.

Tip: combine this with in-page links (<a href="#section-id">) and html { scroll-behavior: smooth; } for smooth scrolling between sections.

Hover Effects (Cards)

These are generic card hover effects. Replace the text with anything: images, icons, or content.

Lift + Shadow
Lift on Hover
Card rises slightly with stronger shadow. Great for clickable tiles or feature boxes.
Classes: hover-card hover-lift
Zoom In
Zoom on Hover
Light scale-up animation. Easy to combine with images or thumbnails.
Classes: hover-card hover-zoom
3D Tilt
Tilt on Hover
Subtle 3D tilt effect using rotate3d. Feels interactive without being too much.
Classes: hover-card hover-tilt
Blurred Background
Blurred Glass
Uses backdrop-filter to blur whatever is behind the card (supported in modern browsers).
Classes: hover-card hover-blur-bg
