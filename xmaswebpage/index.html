Holiday Web Template

A ready-to-use Christmas theme with blinking background lights, glowing links, and festive buttons. Replace the sample URLs and text with your own content.

Seasonal Mode: ON

Links that glow like Christmas lights

These links have a “bulb” indicator and a pulsing glow. Add as many as you want.
Home Shop Events Contact
Tip: If you want the links to “blink” more aggressively, reduce the animation durations in @keyframes textGlow and @keyframes twinkle.

Buttons that light up

These buttons have animated “bulb beads” around the edge. One is candy-cane themed.
Tip: To make the buttons quieter, reduce the opacity in .xmas-btn::before and lower the glow (box-shadow) values.
